Research

The primary focus of my research is on generative simulation for embodied AI. In the era of building robotics with artificial general intelligence (AGI), data is essential as it empowers the development and evaluation of learning-based algorithms. However, acquiring data and labels is more expensive in robotics settings, especially considering the failure cost in corner cases. To address this issue, we resort to generative AIs, which have revolutionized the way we approach simulations. My specific research interest lies in:

  • Generative models (paritularly, diffusion models) and their application in different representations, which helps us to formulate the generation and editing of images, point clouds, videos, neural fields and so on.
  • 3D reconstruction and graphics (particularly, NeRFs, 3DGS and 4DGS), which helps us to initialize a realistic scenario for editing.
  • Closed-loop evaluation and training between generative simulators (as world dynamic models) and policies (such as end-to-end autonomous driving systems).

I have published 7 papers at top-level conferences such as ICCV, ECCV, ICRA and SIGGRAPH (with 4 papers as first or co-first authors). Please see my Google Scholar profile for details. Really welcome fellow researchers to exchange ideas with me :)
